It's not just a coin. It's more like a movement.


The Machinecoin (3 letter short form often used in public: “MAC”) is a free and decentralized peer-to-peer cryptocurrency that was created on the basis of Litecoin and Bitcoin. The Machinecoin is completely open source and uses scrypt as a proof-of-work algorithm. To get started just either compile the given source code by yourself or grab a pre-compiled package and configure it accordingly to your needs.
